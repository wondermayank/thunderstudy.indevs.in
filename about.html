Quality education should not cost money. ThunderStudy provides free notes, mock tests and downloadable books for CBSE Class 12, CUET 2026, JEE, NEET, NDA, CLAT/AILET, CA, NCERT (Class 6-12), Diploma courses, TET/CTET, CAT/MBA, SSC, Banking and UPSC — completely free, always. No paywall. No subscription. No hidden charges.

ThunderStudy collects zero data. All progress, scores, and preferences are stored locally in your browser. Nothing is ever sent to any server. There are no cookies, no trackers, and no analytics. Your data never leaves your device.
