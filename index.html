To prepare for CUET in 2 months: Week 1–2 — complete NCERT revision for all domain subjects. Week 3–4 — practice chapter-wise important questions available on ThunderStudy. Week 5–6 — take full-length CUET mock tests at commercesehoga.github.io/cuet. Week 7–8 — revise weak areas, focus on GAT (General Aptitude Test) and English language section. Take at least 10 full mocks before the exam. Daily 4–5 hours of focused study is sufficient if your NCERT base is strong.

Computer Science (CS) is more theory and programming intensive — it covers Python programming, data structures, computer networks and cybersecurity. Informatics Practices (IP) is more applied — it focuses on Python basics, data handling using Pandas and NumPy, data visualisation using Matplotlib, databases with MySQL, and societal impacts of technology. CS is generally considered harder than IP. Both are CBSE Class 12 electives and are also available as CUET domain subjects. Study material for both is at commercesehoga.github.io/computer/cs and /computer/ip.
